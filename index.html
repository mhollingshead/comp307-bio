Project Idea

Though I would be open to working on any of the project topics, I chose to list the Online Marketplace as my preferred topic.

With my experience in API integration and front-end UI development, this topic stands out to me as one that I would best be able to contribute to.

I feel that there's a lot of room for creativity with a project like this, and even though I'm open to any ideas, I think the perfect one came to me in a dream several years ago.

I'm talking, of course, about onesock.com. I'm sure we've all been there– we have the perfect pair of socks, we're wearing them constantly, everything's going swimmingly until one day, one of them disappears!

Usually at this point, our singular sock is renderred useless– until onesock.com came along. With onesock.com, users can either find their missing sock to complete their favorite pair or make some extra cash off of their once-useless singular sock.

Never again will an incomplete pair of socks be thrown away or forgotten. Find your missing sock. onesock.com.

Because this idea is a running joke I have with myself, I'm obviously open to other ideas. I think there are a lot of creative paths we can take with a topic like this and I'm excited to see what kind of useful tool we can come up with!
